# Goal Probability and Monte Carlo Simulation

Goal probability estimates how often a portfolio reaches a target amount across many simulated market paths.

Key ideas:
- A 44% goal probability means the target was reached in 44% of simulations.
- Median accumulated amount shows the middle outcome across simulations.
- A plan can be close to the target in dollar terms even if the formal probability of fully reaching the target is still below 50%.

The strongest levers for improving goal probability are:
- increasing monthly contribution
- extending the investing horizon
- lowering the goal amount
- accepting a more growth-oriented risk profile when appropriate
